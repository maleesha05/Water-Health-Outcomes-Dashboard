## Water-Health-Outcomes-Dashboard
This project explores the relationship between water &amp; sanitation access (SDG 6) and health outcomes (SDG 3), particularly focusing on disease spread and child mortality. In here the goal was to identify patterns and show outcomes through an interactive data visualization dashboard.

**Tools & Technologies:**
- Power BI (Dashboard)
- Excel / Python (Data cleaning & merging)

**Objectives:**
- Analyze how access to clean water impacts health
- Identify patterns across countries and years
- Provide clear, interactive visual insights

**Dashboard Features:**
- Line Chart – Trends over time
- Choropleth Map – Country-wise comparison
- Scatter Plot – Relationship between variables
- Bar Chart – Top vs Bottom countries
- Slicers – Interactive filtering

**Key Tasks:**
- Consolidated and combined several CSV files into a single dataset
- Addressed missing data and maintained dataset consistency
- Examined patterns in mortality rates across various regions
- Developed a dashboard to visualize global health trends
- Shared results and insights through a collaborative presentation

**Key Insights:**
- Regions with restricted access to clean water show higher mortality rates
- Clear link between sanitation standards and overall public health
- Data emphasizes the importance of better infrastructure and policy measures

**Conclusion:**
This dashboard highlights the critical role of clean water in improving global health and supports the importance of sustainable development goals
